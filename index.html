Matthew E. Smith DDS

Dr. Matt prides himself on delivering comfortable, personalized care. He is sincerely dedicated to maintaining a high level of knowledge and skill in the ever-changing field of dentistry. His education did not stop after graduating from Dental School. He is a continuous student and spends a tremendous amount of time each year to stay on top of the latest advances for cosmetic and general dentists. He has completed the comprehensive continuing dental education program offered by the Kois Center located in Seattle, Washington. The nine-course prestigious curriculum requires over 200 hours of continuing education encompassing all clinical areas of dentistry; gums, teeth, bite problems, cosmetics and implants involving both didactic and hands-on training. Dr. Matt sustains delicate skills in providing dentistry with a focus on both function and cosmetics. He is a certified Invisalign provider, offering patients a clear, aesthetic way to align teeth.

Dr. Matthew Smith has gone through extensive training in Sedation Dentistry area and has safely, successfully and more importantly comfortably treated a number of fearful patients using this 1 pill sedation dentistry technique. This incredible technique allows those patients who have avoided the dentist for years to comfortably undergo treatment they may have avoided for years while they comfortably snooze in the chair.

Dr. Matt remains closely involved within the dental community by attending both monthly & annual meetings with various organizations. Dr. Matt serves at Faculty Clinic Instructor at Marquette University School of Dentistry and served as a faculty clinic instructor at MATC hygiene program. He volunteers at clinics for underserved children and participates in "Give Kids a Smile" and "Mission of Mercy" programs.

Dr. Matt and his wife Jenny are proud parents to Logan, Kylie and Isabel. Besides spending time with his wife and children, he enjoys boating, cycling, hiking, camping, photography and travel.

Dental Implants

Dental implants with artificial teeth are the closest thing to regrowing your natural teeth. They are strong, stable, durable and virtually undetectable. By filling gaps left by missing teeth, implants can provide better chewing ability and prevent jaw joint problems. They are far superior to removable dentures.

In the event that more than one tooth is missing, several implants may provide a base for a series of artificial teeth known as a fixed bridge. Implants can even be used to secure a full set of removable dentures for people who have no remaining natural teeth.

Comprehensive Services

Digital Impressions

Our use of digital x-ray technology and Cadent iTero® technology reduces your radiation exposure and eliminates the use of chemicals. An accurate impression is fundamental to a successful outcome in restorative dentistry.

We can see and treat problems long before they become visible on traditional x-rays. The increased precision means saving you time and money and avoiding complications with your treatments.

Sedation Dentistry

Patients who are apprehensive about receiving dental treatment often avoid going to the dentist. Ultimately, this avoidance can lead to advanced disease and the need for expensive treatments that could have been avoided. Sedation Dentistry provides a safe, effective way to alleviate dental anxiety and provide patients with needed treatment.
